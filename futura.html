Typography Poster


DES16 poster project. Challenge was to design a poster based off a particular typeface. I chose to dedicate my work to Futura, a modern font often used in both headlines and body text. The design is inspired from one of the original artists who utilized this typeface. Created using Adobe Illustrator.
